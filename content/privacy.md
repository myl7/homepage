## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how myl7.org ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles your information when you visit our website.

This website is not directed at individuals in the European Union (EU) or the European Economic Area (EEA), and we do not comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
If you are located in the EU/EEA, please be aware that by using this website, the protections afforded by the GDPR may not apply.

## Information We Collect

We use the following third-party analytics services to collect anonymous usage data:

### Google Analytics

Google Analytics collects information such as how often you visit the site, what pages you view, and what other sites you visited prior to coming to this site.
Google Analytics uses cookies to identify returning visitors.
The information collected is aggregated and anonymous.
For more details, see the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Cloudflare Web Analytics

Cloudflare Web Analytics collects page view data and performance metrics.
It is a privacy-first analytics tool that does not use client-side state (such as cookies or localStorage) to collect usage metrics.
For more details, see the [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

### Vercel Analytics

Vercel Analytics collects anonymous usage and performance data, including page views and web vitals.
For more details, see the [Vercel Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy).

## How We Use Your Information

The data collected through the analytics services listed above is used solely to understand website traffic and improve the user experience.
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to any third party.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any collected data with third parties beyond the analytics service providers listed above, which process data on our behalf.

## Cookies

Some of the analytics services we use may place cookies on your device.
You can configure your browser to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
